* Directives:
   Directives are instructions to DOM, we can add diectives with attribute selector

* [ngStyle] allows us to dynamically add styles
* [ngClass] allows us to dynamically remove classes


* Two types of directives, Attribute Directives and Structural Directives
* Attribute Directive- it only affects the element they are added eg, adding background color to an element
* Structural Directive - it affect the whole area in the DOM eg, ngIf
